OrderFlow - The Sensex index itself doesn't have an order book - only its 30 underlying stocks do. This system reconstructs Sensex bid/ask book by weight-aggregation the depth and trades of each constituent. On top of that book, it computes order-flow indicators traders normally only have for single stocks(OBI, CVD, VPIN) but at the index level, plus options-market positioning(GEX, IV skew, Max Pain), and rolls them into a composite trade signal.

An AI coach named Niki narrates the journey, generates personalized quests, and holds long-term memory about who you're trying to become.

WDT(Weighted decision Trading) - A majority voting regime conditional weighted decision matrix that:
  Dynamically adjust strategy weights based on market regime
  Extracts 28 hand selected strategy variants from the 6 indicators
  Produces a continuous composite signal in -1 to +1
  Applies decorrelation penalties to redundant strategy pairs
